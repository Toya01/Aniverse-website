Community Rules

General Guidelines

1. Be Respectful

Treat all members with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind will be tolerated.

2. Keep it Clean

No NSFW content, excessive profanity, or inappropriate discussions. Keep the chat family-friendly.

3. No Spam

Avoid excessive messages, repeated content, or unnecessary mentions. Use appropriate channels for your content.

Content Guidelines

1. Anime & Gaming Focus

Keep discussions related to anime, gaming, and community events. Use appropriate channels for off-topic discussions.

2. Art & Media

Share your art and media in designated channels. Credit original creators when sharing others' work.

3. Voice Channels

Be mindful of others in voice channels. Keep noise levels reasonable and respect quiet hours.

Event Participation

1. Fair Play

No cheating or exploiting in gaming events. Play fair and respect other participants.

2. Giveaways

Follow giveaway rules carefully. No alt accounts or rule manipulation will be tolerated.

3. Event Behavior

Be sportsmanlike during events. Respect moderators and follow event-specific rules.

Important Note

Failure to follow these rules may result in warnings, temporary mutes, or permanent bans depending on the severity of the violation. Moderators have the final say in all situations.
