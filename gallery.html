Welcome to ANIVERSE

This is the welcome message that we send to the community when they join the server. We do this to make them feel welcome and to help them get to know the community.

Community Events

Join our exciting community events! From gaming tournaments and anime watch parties to art contests and music nights. We host regular events to bring our community together and create memorable experiences. Check our events channel for upcoming activities!

Nitro Drops

Nitro Drops are a way for the community to get a chance to get a nitro for free. We do this by having a giveaway every month. The giveaway is open to everyone and the winner will be chosen by a random number generator.

Art Contest

Show off your favorite anime characters at our annual cosplay showcase! Join fellow cosplayers in a celebration of creativity and fandom. Share your costumes, participate in competitions, and win exciting prizes. Whether you're a seasoned cosplayer or just starting out, there's a place for everyone in our cosplay community!

Music Night

Join our music night every week! We have a variety of music genres and we play them all night long. We also have a music bot that we use to play the music. You can request a song and we will play it for you.
